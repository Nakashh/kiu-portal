Our Story

Built for the Bold

Kutaisi International University is a private higher education institution founded with a clear mission: to deliver world-class education in Georgia through innovative teaching, research, and community engagement.

Our modern campus in Kutaisi—Georgia's second largest city and ancient capital—offers students a vibrant intellectual and cultural environment, backed by strong ties with European and international partner institutions.
